# Vehicle-Loan-Calculator---Assignment-1

A simple Android app that helps you estimate your vehicle loan details.  
Enter the vehicle price, down payment, loan period, and yearly interest rate, and the app will calculate:

- Loan amount
- Total interest
- Total payment
- Monthly payment
